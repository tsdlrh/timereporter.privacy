Time Reporter — Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Time Reporter App is a meeting timer and reporting assistant designed for Toastmasters clubs. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your choices regarding your information.

2. Summary

  • The App works offline for timing and storing meeting data on your device.
  • We do not sell your personal data or show third‑party ads.
  • You can delete all data within the app at any time.

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Local meeting & speech dataMeeting name, date, speaker name , actual duration, rule used, overtimeProvide core functionality: timing, reports, analytics
Rule configurationUser-created timing rules, thresholds, alert preferencesCustom time rule
Local report dataReport name, date, generate timeAnalysis speech time performance

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To run the core functionality of the App (timing, recording, generating reports).

5. Data Storage & Security

All meeting and speech records are stored locally on your device.

6. Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

7. Your Choices & Rights

  • Delete data: You can delete all app data from within the app settings.

8. Children

We do not collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us to request deletion.
